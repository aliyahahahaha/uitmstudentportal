Bachelor of Information Science

The Bachelor of Information Science (Hons) program at UiTM (Universiti Teknologi MARA) is designed to provide students with the knowledge and skills necessary to work in the fields of information management, technology, and systems. The program typically covers areas such as information systems, data management, digital technologies, programming, web development, and network administration.

Admission Requirements:

  • STPM/Matriculation/Pre-University: Usually requires a minimum CGPA or specific subject grades (e.g., in mathematics and sciences).
  • Diploma: For those with a relevant diploma, there may be an option to enter at an advanced level.
Bachelor of Business Administration

The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) (Hons) program at UiTM (Universiti Teknologi MARA) is designed to provide students with a solid foundation in business and management principles, preparing them for leadership and decision-making roles in various industries.

Admission Requirements:

STPM/Matriculation/Pre-University: A minimum CGPA or specific subject grades (e.g., in business, economics, or mathematics).

Diploma: If you have a related diploma, you may enter at an advanced level.

Bachelor of Engineering

The Bachelor of Engineering (BEng) program at UiTM (Universiti Teknologi MARA) is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in engineering principles, preparing them for a career in various engineering fields. UiTM offers different engineering specializations, such as civil, mechanical, electrical, and chemical engineering.

Admission Requirements:

STPM/Matriculation/Pre-University: Typically requires a minimum CGPA or specific subject grades, including mathematics and science subjects such as physics or chemistry.

Diploma: For those with a relevant diploma, there may be an option to enter at an advanced level or transfer to a related engineering program.

Bachelor of Arts

The Bachelor of Arts (BA) (Hons) program at UiTM (Universiti Teknologi MARA) offers a diverse range of specializations within the arts and humanities fields. This program is designed to equip students with critical thinking, creativity, and communication skills, which are essential in various industries such as media, design, social sciences, and education.

Admission Requirements:

STPM/Matriculation/Pre-University: A minimum CGPA or specific subject grades (often including subjects like English, social sciences, or arts).

Diploma: Students with a relevant diploma may be eligible for advanced standing in related specializations.
